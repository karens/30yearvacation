About This Site

Retirement sounds great. You quit work, play golf, and travel, and have no responsibilities. Who wouldn't love it? But I could easily spend 30 or more years in retirement, a virtual 30 year "vacation".

Does that idea of retirement still make sense? Can I afford to support myself for 30 years without a pay check? How can anyone plan and save for all the things that might happen over 30 years? When would anyone ever be "ready" to retire? Can I do something meaningful to make it more than a vacation? This site is my attempt to find answers to my questions about my own "30 year vacation", whenever, and if ever, that happens.
