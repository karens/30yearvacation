Creative Solutions to Fund Your New Business https://30-year-vacation-8.lndo.site/blog-posts/beware-creative-solutions-fund-your-new-business <span>Beware!! Creative Solutions to Fund Your New Business</span> <span><span>Karen Stevenson</span></span> <span>Thu, 07/04/2013 - 6:21 pm</span> <div class="field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item"><p>It's sad to see someone get to retirement without enough money to make it through. It's even sadder to see someone save all their life and then lose it needlessly. Many Boomers realize that retirement may be the perfect time to start a new business, maybe that business you've always dreamed of owning. But Boomers have two things that make them attractive targets for schemes that may or may not be in their best interest: they may have a pool of cash that they saved up for retirement and some significant equity in their home.</p><p>I've been seeing pitches lately that suggest that Boomers who want to start a business or buy a franchise do so using money from their retirement accounts. Ordinarily you may not be able to withdraw the money you need without paying taxes on it, but a new program called Rollover as Business Start-Ups (ROBS) allows you to do it, apparently, without tax consequences.</p><p>Be very cautious about this. It's not clear that the IRS is going to allow it, and if they rule against it there will be fees and penalties to pay. It's also not clear how it can be unwound later, for instance, to pass the business on to a child. Avoid doing this if you have any other way to finance the business. And even if you don't be sure to get a second, and maybe even a third, opinion from someone who is not trying to sell this program. I was a CPA for many years, and I wouldn't recommend it to anyone.</p><p>You worked hard to accumlate that money. Don't take any chances with it!</p></div> <div class="field field--name-field-tags field--type-entity-reference field--label-hidden field--items"> <div class="field--item"><a href="https://30-year-vacation-8.lndo.site/tags/starting-business" hreflang="en">Starting a business</a></div> <div class="field--item"><a href="https://30-year-vacation-8.lndo.site/tags/401k" hreflang="en">401(k)</a></div> </div> Thu, 04 Jul 2013 23:21:34 +0000 Karen Stevenson 37 at https://30-year-vacation-8.lndo.site
